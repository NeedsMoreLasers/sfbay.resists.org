The world around you is not what it seems…

Ingress is a location-based game of capture-the-flag played on smartphones. Resistance Agents explore the real world around them, and the competition is waged locally, regionally, and worldwide, over real landmarks, art, history, and recreation. It’s a fun battle, and can be very active, social, and even educational!

Join the Resistance and Save the World from alien ingression!

1) Download Ingress, choose your agent name and faction wisely, and try the training missions (Android / iOS)

2) Then explore your neighborhood with your phone, and the intel map at https://ingress.com/intel (click here)

3) Get gameplay tips, and coordinate with your local Resistance teammates on G+ and Hangouts (click here)
